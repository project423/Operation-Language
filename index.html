Acacdemic Language Therapy

Dyslexia therapy differes from other dyslexia tutoring by treating the neurological components which make up dyslexia. At Operation Lanuage, students receive customized structured literacy to activate the areas of the brain designed for reading and learning.

Testing and Evaluations

Operation Language evaluates for dyslexia and other language based disorders. Evaluations include normed tests and include informal data such as parent and teacher questionnaires. Customized lessons are also available for other teachers who work with students identified as dyslexic.
